# Outreach Goals

## Primary Goal

Increase exposure for Welcome Home Haiti by earning podcast interviews, media mentions, newsletter placements, social follows, social shares, and warm introductions.

## Success Metrics

- 15 personalized outreach emails queued or sent per active outreach day
- response status captured for each contacted lead
- social follows or shares gained from aligned creators
- podcast guest invitations or exploratory calls scheduled
- warm introductions to churches, mission leaders, business leaders, or donor-minded communities
- new research contacts added with clear source and fit notes

## Priority Audiences

- Christian podcast hosts
- mission and nonprofit leadership podcasts
- humanitarian and social impact podcasts
- Haiti-focused or international relief creators
- Christian business and leadership podcasts
- church networks and ministry communicators
- creators with audiences that care about generosity, discipleship, orphan care, poverty, employment, or community transformation

## Best Story Angles

- Homes, jobs, and discipleship working together
- Haitian staff and local leadership showing up consistently
- Peterson-style transformation through mentorship, work, faith, and home ownership
- Northern Haiti hope story without making crisis the whole story
- Donors and partners as participants in real stories, not as helpers of an organization

## Win Definition

A win is not only a booked interview. A new follower, share, warm reply, introduction, or permission to follow up is meaningful progress.
