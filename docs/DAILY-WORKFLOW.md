# Daily Outreach Workflow

## Daily Cycle

1. Select up to 15 best-fit contacts from the queue.
2. Research or verify each lead enough to personalize the email.
3. Draft each message with a specific reason for reaching out.
4. Review the batch before sending.
5. Send only approved messages.
6. Record send date, subject, and status.
7. Monitor replies and update outcomes.
8. Produce a short daily summary for Neil.

## Status Values

- `research`: needs more review before contact
- `ready`: ready for draft generation
- `drafted`: email generated but not approved
- `approved`: approved for sending
- `sent`: outreach email sent
- `follow_up_due`: no reply after follow-up window
- `interested`: positive response or invitation
- `scheduled`: call/interview scheduled
- `completed`: interview or placement completed
- `declined`: not interested
- `bounced`: delivery failed
- `do_not_contact`: opt-out, complaint, or explicit no-contact

## Reply Review

Replies should be summarized back to Neil with:

- sender/show name
- response status
- recommended next action
- suggested reply draft if needed
- deadline or urgency if present

## Follow-Up Timing

Default follow-up window: 7 to 10 days after first email unless the contact replied, bounced, or declined.
